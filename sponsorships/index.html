Sponsorships

For Sponsors

Your sponsorship of this premier conference is a strong statement about your organization’s commitment to the field of HCI, as well as your commitment to the professional development of your staff. CSCW 2022 continues to offer new and exciting opportunities for sponsors. Benefits include publicity and professional development as well as recruiting, networking, and business development.

For more detailed information about these and the many other advantages of sponsoring, please contact sponsor2022@cscw.acm.org. Participation in the Sponsorship program is an ideal opportunity to demonstrate your organization’s commitment to the field of HCI, and CSCW 2022 offers many opportunities for your organization to make thousands of delegates aware of your commitment to high-quality interaction design.

For Recruiters

CSCW 2022 provides special support for recruiting by offering organizations the opportunity to engage with delegates for this purpose through exhibiting.
